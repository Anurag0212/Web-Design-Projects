The Yurts at Pacific Trail

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground.
Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that
can be opened

How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurts is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quit and gas-fired stove
The luxury camping experience also includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold
running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the fridge

What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable
packing shoes and plan to dress for changing weathers with layers of clothing
